defination of causitive verb
_________________________________
ekhane sub nije nije kichu korena borong kawke korte dey ba kawke diye kichu koriye ney / setai causitive verb.
  jei verb er maddome subj, obj ke diye kono kaj koriye ney tai causitive verb.


rules 
_________
subj + causitive verb mane main verb + person mane obj + v1 + etc..
subj + causitive verb mane main verb + things mane bostu + v3 + etc..

// tense onusare   get/ am getting/ have got / will get / will be getting / got etc... boste pare.
   // mane causitive verb main verb hisabe bosbe, just cousitive verb er ekhane tense change hobe. 

make = jodi kajta korte take baddo kora hoy ba request kora hoy tahole make hobe.
            // i make him do the work.

get = bujiye sujiye ba raji ba convince koriye kono kichi korale get bosbe. 
           // i get him to do the work.
                   / get use korle infinitive to use korte hobe.

have = jodi kaj ta kora tar daitto hoy and ami take diye eta koriye nei tahole have hobe.
            // i have him do the work.

help = kawake sahajjo sense kichu bolte.
           // i help him do the work.        mane ami take kaj ti korte sahajjo kori.
let, allow, permit = jodi kawke kono kichu korte onumuti dewa hoy tahole egular je kono ekta bosano jabe.
                         // i let him do the work     or   i allow him to do the work   or i permit him to do the work.
                            / allow and permit er por get er moto infinitive to bosaite hobe.

// ei niyome sob causitive verb active and passive hobe.  let passive hisabe use kora jabena.
active : i get him to do the work.    / ami take diye kajta korai.
passive : i get the work done by him.  / ami tar dara kajta koriye nei.

active : i make him do the work. 
passive : i make the work done by him.


active : i allow him do the work .  // ami take kajta korte dei.
passive : he is allowed to do the work by me.  // take kajta korte dewa hoy amar dara.
